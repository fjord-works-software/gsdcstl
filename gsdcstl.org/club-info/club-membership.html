HOW TO JOIN THE GERMAN SHEPHERD DOG CLUB OF ST. LOUIS, Inc.

CLICK HERE FOR MEMBERSHIP APPLICATION 

Questions: please contact the Recording Secretary, Laura Marrocco.

In order to be considered for membership, you must:

Turn in the application (with two sponsors) to the recording secretary or a club officer if she/he is absent at that meeting.

Yearly dues must accompany the application 
($20 for individuals or $30 for families) .

Don't forget to sign the Guest Book (at the club meeting)

Your application will be read at the next two club meetings.

After the second reading, your application will be voted on by the general membership at that meeting.
